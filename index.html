Visual attention • Adaptation

    Hi there!

    I’m a vision scientist studying how perception and attention interact over space and time. A lot of my work uses psychophysics to ask about the representational format and content of perception, particularly how the internal structure of perceptual representations enables our experience of a coherent, stable world. and how it stays stable even when objects move.

    I’m especially interested in adaptation and reference frames (retinotopic vs. object‑based/spatiotopic), and in the relationship between sensory traces and working memory.
